Turn off third-party trackers

This feature is opt-in because many creators use Google, Facebook, and third-party analytics to track traffic and conversions. If you don't use anything other than Gumroad's analytics, this setting may be right for you.

The benefits

The costs
Earn extra trust from customers by respecting their privacy This setting disables the Google Analytics and Facebook Pixel integrations, if in use.
Increase the loading speed of product and profile pages The setting precludes using third-party analytics on Gumroad pages.
Continue using Gumroad analytics unaffected, as they are collected server-side. Our internal analytics will take a hit, but that's okay. We aren't trying to impress anyone.

Disabling Non-Essential Cookies

Creators can disable non-essential cookies in three quick steps.

1) Go to the Third-party analytics tab in your Settings

2) Toggle off "Enable third-party analytics services" to disable cookies

3) Click "Update settings"

For more on analytics and tracking, check out Third-party analytics
