You can also find your product URL from the Share tab:

From the Share tab:


Simply click Share while editing your product and click Copy URL

Changing or Customizing your Product's URL

You can change your product's URL by going to the URL box of the product and altering the URL's suffix, or "Product ID". Type over the automatic ID and click "Save Changes" to create a custom URL.

You can update this link at any time, but your previous custom URL will not redirect to the new custom URL.

Your default Gumroad product link will continue to work as well.

Best Practices

  • Use lowercase letters and dashes (-) for clarity.
  • Keep it short and descriptive.
  • Avoid using sensitive or misleading terms.
