How do I give a product as a gift?

When you get to the checkout form, click the “Give as a gift” toggle to allow for gifting.

Just below the recipient's email, you will also see the option to send a personal message to the person you are gifting.

Once you’ve gifted the product successfully, we’ll email you a receipt and the person you gifted it to will receive an email with the product’s download link and your personalized message.

To send a previously purchased item as a gift, simply return to the URL of the product, click “Purchase again”, and toggle the gift option to send as a gift.

Gifting Membership

You can gift a membership to someone for the first charge of a certain subscription period (1 month, 3 months, etc.). The membership will not auto-renew. The gift recipient must update the payment method from their receipt to renew the membership. 

Restrictions

  • You cannot gift more than one product in the cart at a time
